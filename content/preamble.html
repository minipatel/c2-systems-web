Command and Control (C2) is an overloaded term. This website deals with C2 in the military context. Smart people have already worked long and hard on defining what these words mean and the Wiki article sums it up nicely.

Onthis site, you will find content about the problem space of military C2 and specifically the technological solutions to address this.
As it turns out the fundamental problems of C2 does not differ much from those of banks, webshops and online games. The problem is that sometimes this realization gets lost in a myriad of functional requirements and the application ultimately end up as a big ball of mud. For this reason the philosophy behind my software project was a challenge to take an architecture first approach - secondly, use only free open source libraries - and thirdly, see if that may end up solving some functional problems in the business domain along the way ...
